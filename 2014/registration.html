Registration



Notes

1. Every paper needs to have at least one author registered to be included in the conference proceedings.
2. Each paper must be fully paid for.
3. The exception to note 2 is: If you have one or more posters accepted, the registration fee for the second poster (if you have only posters accepted), or each poster (if you have full or short papers accepted) is the half of the listed amount.
4. Student rate is used only if the student is the presenter and is the first author of the paper.
5. Refund Policy: No refund will be given for this conference if your registration is cancelled.

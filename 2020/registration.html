Virtual event

Due to the growing number of COVID-19 cases and the resulting restrictions in The Netherlands, we have decided not to host VINCI2020 on-site in Eindhoven. Instead, the conference will be held in a virtual manner using the video conferencing platform Hopin.

The conference is over, thanks for participating!


Registration for authors

As the VINCI 2020 conference will be held in a virtual manner this year, registration is a bit different. We require at least one author of an accepted paper or poster to register for the conference at a reduced rate. The attendance of other participants will be free.

The registration fee for VINCI 2020 is the same for every author: €100. The deadline for registration will be November 14, 2020.

Registration

Registration Policy

Each accepted paper or poster must be covered by at least one author's registration as “presenter” (not “student participant”) to be included in the conference proceedings. If one author will make more than one presentation, the registration fees are required as the same number of presentations.

Registration Fees
  • $480 for early presenter or participant (ACM Member rate) ($620 after July 25, 2016)
  • $580 for early presenter participant (Non-ACM Member rate) ($720 after July 25, 2016)
  • $280 for early student participant (ACM Student Member rate) ($375 after July 25, 2016)
  • $350 for early student participant (Non-ACM Student Member rate) ($475 after July 25, 2016)


Note:
The registration fee includes the reception on 24th and banquet on 25th, conference proceedings, and attendance to keynote speeches and all technical sessions.
